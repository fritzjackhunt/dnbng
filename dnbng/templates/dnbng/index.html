Got questions, we are here to help

You may need a D-U-N-S Number to :

  • Apply for government grants or contracts
  • Create a business profile with D&B for credibility and visibility
  • Apply for Apple and Google Play Sore developer programs

Your D-U-N-S Number comes out after two working days but it takes 7-14 days to reflect across the global database

Typically, you'll need

  • Registered business name
  • Business address and contact details
  • Name of the business owner or principal
  • Comapany registration certificate (CAC in Nigeria)
  • Tax identification number (if available)

D-U-N-S Numbers are only issued to registered entities. If you are a sole proprietor, your business must be legally registered before you can apply

Dun & Bradstreet does not issue certificates, but we will provide you with your official D-U-N-S Number and confirmation email once approved
